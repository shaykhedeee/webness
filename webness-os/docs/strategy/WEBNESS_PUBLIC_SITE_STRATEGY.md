# Webness Public Site Strategy

Date: June 4, 2026

## Decision

Webness is one parent brand: a founder-led digital systems studio for premium businesses. Signal Room is the proprietary diagnostic and tracking engine inside Webness.

Do not position the public site as an AI agency. Position it as:

> Websites, apps, and growth systems built to perform.

## Public Funnel

1. Founder content, referrals, portfolio proof, or outbound.
2. Public website visit.
3. Apply for Signal Scan or view work.
4. Paid Signal Scan.
5. Strategy call and priority map.
6. Website/app/build sprint proposal.
7. Growth System or Premium Systems retainer.
8. Signal Room tracks improvement after launch.

## Page Strategy

- Homepage: premium positioning, portfolio proof, build lanes, Signal Room preview, Webness 100, offer ladder, process, founder proof, FAQ, final CTA.
- Work: proof categories and case-study details.
- Services: clear offer ladder and service detail pages.
- Signal Room: explain diagnosis -> priority map -> sprint -> retainer.
- Apply: application form with budget, problem, desired outcome, and timeline.

## Public CTA Rule

Every primary path should lead to `/apply` unless the user is reviewing work or service fit.
