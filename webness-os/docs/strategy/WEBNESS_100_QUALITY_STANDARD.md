# Webness 100 Quality Standard

Date: June 4, 2026

## Public Site Standard

- Lighthouse target: 95+ for SEO, accessibility, and best practices.
- No key page below 90.
- Mobile layouts must not overlap or overflow.
- First viewport must show brand, offer, proof, and CTA within 5 seconds.
- Every main CTA should lead to Signal Scan/application.
- No fake metrics or unsupported ROI claims.

## Offer Standard

- Every service page includes problem, outcome, deliverables, price, timeline, proof, and CTA.
- Every case study includes challenge, Webness role, scope, decisions, and proof links.
- Every accepted offer targets 60%+ gross margin.
- Every client path routes to paid scan, sprint, or retainer.

## Review Cadence

Run this checklist before any public launch, major copy update, or paid outreach campaign.
