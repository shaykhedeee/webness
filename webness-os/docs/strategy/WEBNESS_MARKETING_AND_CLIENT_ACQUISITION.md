# Webness Marketing And Client Acquisition

Date: June 4, 2026

## Client Acquisition Strategy

Use portfolio-led founder content and targeted outbound.

## Weekly Content

Publish two founder-led posts per week:

- Website or app teardown.
- Before/after portfolio breakdown.
- "Why this business stopped performing."
- Signal Room priority map example.

## Monthly Outbound

Send 100 targeted messages per month to businesses with visible digital issues.

Target:

- Ecommerce and DTC brands.
- Healthcare and clinics.
- Tech and manufacturing companies.
- Interior, construction, and premium local services.
- Founder-led businesses with proof but weak digital systems.

## Outreach Principle

Use portfolio and specific observations. Do not send generic "we build websites" messages.

## Offer

The outreach CTA should be:

> I can run a paid Signal Scan and tell you the highest-leverage next move before you spend on a redesign, app, or marketing retainer.
