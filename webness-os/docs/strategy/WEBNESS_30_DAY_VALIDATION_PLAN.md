# Webness 30-Day Validation Plan

Date: June 4, 2026

## Goal

Validate that the Webness public positioning can generate paid Signal Scans and at least one build sprint.

## Targets

- 100 qualified prospects identified.
- 40 personalized outreach messages sent.
- 10 discovery/application responses.
- 5 paid Signal Scan pitches.
- 3 paid Signal Scans closed.
- 1 Build Sprint closed.

## Decision Rule

If Webness does not close 3 paid Signal Scans and 1 Build Sprint, do not build more features. Refine niche, proof, offer, pricing, or outreach message first.

## Weekly Rhythm

- Week 1: Launch public site and prepare prospect list.
- Week 2: Send first 20 messages and publish 2 teardown posts.
- Week 3: Send next 20 messages and pitch paid Signal Scan.
- Week 4: Close scans, review objections, and adjust positioning.
