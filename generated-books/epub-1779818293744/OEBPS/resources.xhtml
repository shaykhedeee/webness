ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Webness Botanical Lab is at the forefront of empowering plant enthusiasts to cultivate thriving indoor ecosystems through profound understanding and intuitive connection. Founded on the principle that true plant mastery transcends rote rules, Webness Botanical Lab specializes in demystifying plant physiology, environmental adaptation, and plant communication. Their innovative "Rooted Understanding" pedagogy guides individuals to "read" their green companions' subtle signals, fostering a sustainable and deeply rewarding relationship with nature indoors. With a unique blend of scientific expertise and a passion for biophilic living, Webness Botanical Lab equips you to move beyond basic care and transform your home into a vibrant, responsive, and intuitively managed indoor jungle. Their mission is to cultivate not just beautiful spaces, but a deeper, more sustainable connection between humans and the botanical world.
