RESOURCES

To further your journey towards "Rooted Understanding" and cultivate your dream indoor jungle, Webness Botanical Lab recommends the following resources:

  • Accurate Digital Hygrometer/Thermometer: Essential for precisely monitoring your home's microclimates. Look for models with min/max memory and good accuracy.
  • Reliable Moisture Meter: Move beyond guesswork. A quality moisture meter helps you understand soil moisture levels deep within the pot, preventing over or underwatering.
  • High-Quality Pruning Shears/Snips: Sharp, clean tools are crucial for healthy plant maintenance. Invest in a durable pair that fits comfortably in your hand.
  • Local University Cooperative Extension Services: Often overlooked, these resources provide scientifically vetted, region-specific advice on plant care, pest management, and environmental factors.
  • "The Houseplant Expert" by D.G. Hessayon: A classic, comprehensive guide offering detailed information on a vast array of houseplants, their specific needs, and common issues.
  • "Plantopedia: The Definitive Guide to Houseplants" by Lauren Camilleri & Sophia Kaplan: A beautifully illustrated and modern guide that combines practical care advice with stunning botanical design inspiration.
  • The Sill (thesill.com/blog): A fantastic online resource for accessible plant care guides, troubleshooting tips, and inspiration for integrating plants into your home design.
